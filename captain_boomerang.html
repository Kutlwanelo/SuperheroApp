Captain Boomerang (George "Digger" Harkness)

"I'm not doing this out of the goodness of my heart"

Backstory

A selfish, unpredictable thief who somehow got roped into Task Force X. Unlike most squad members, he doesn't care about teamwork-he's just there for survival and whatever profit he can squeeze out

Abilities and Combat Skills

  • Master boomerang thrower.
  • Quick reflexes and unpredictable attack patterns.
  • Sneaky and deceptive personality

Weapons and Gear

  • Razer-sharp boomerangs (some with explosives)
  • Hidden knives for close-range combat.

Memorable Quotes

  • "Hey, well, you know what they say about the crazy ones."
