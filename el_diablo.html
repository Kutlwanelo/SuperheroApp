El Diablo (Chato Santana)

"Ain't nothing to worry about me. I'm cool homie."

Backstory

A former gangster with fire-based superpowers, Chato Santana took a vow to never use his abilities again after accidently destroying his own family. In the squad he struggles between redemption and the violent nature of his past

Abilities and Combat Skills

  • Pyrokinesis : Can summon and control fire.
  • Fire Manipulation Control: Uses flames as deadly attacks.
  • Spiritual strength: Battles inner demons

Weapons and Gear

  • His own flames are his weapon.
  • Tatto markings infused with his supernatural power.
  • Firearms: Proficient in using pistols and machine guns.
  • Explosives: Occasionally uses grenades and other explosives

Memorable Quotes

  • "I already lost one family. I ain't gonna lose another."
  • "Now you're screwed."
  • "I was trying to get you there. Phil Jackson. We good, right?"
