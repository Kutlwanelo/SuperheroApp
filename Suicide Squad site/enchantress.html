Enchantress (June Moone)

"I am more than a dream, June. I am your destiny and you are mine."

Backstory

Enchantress is an ancient, interdimensional with who possesses the body of June Moone, an archaeologist who accidentally freed her from imprisonment. Initially controlled by Amanda Walker, Enchantress later rebels, freeing her brother Incubus and attempting to take over Erth with their combined mystical powers When June Moone unleashed her, she became a split personality, with June struggling to suppress the witch's influence.

Abilities and Combat Skills

  • Mind Control: Influences others to do her bidding.
  • Sorcery & Dark Magic: Can manipulate energy and reality.
  • Teleportation & Transformation: Can shift forms instantly.
  • Superhuman Strength and Durability

Weapons and Gear

  • Mystical Energy Blasts: Can destroy enemies with raw magic.
  • Ancient Rituals: Uses spells to summon creatures and manipulate reality
  • Firearms: Proficient in using pistols and machine guns.

Memorable Quotes

  • "Bow before me, mortals!"
  • "Yes, child. You need only to bow and serve beneath my feet."
  • "The humans turned against us"
