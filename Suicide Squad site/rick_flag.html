Rick Flag

"You disobey me, you die. You try to escape, you die. You otherwise irritate or vex me...and guess what? You also die"

Backstory

A highly skilled military commander, Rick Flag is Amanda Walker's loyal enforcer. Unlike the other squad members, he is a legitimate hero, but his moral struggle in leadin a team of criminals makes his role complicated.
